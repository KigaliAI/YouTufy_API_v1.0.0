1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of text sent to your browser by a website you visit. They help the website remember information about your visit.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • To understand how you use the YouTufy website
  • To improve your browsing experience
  • For basic analytics and functionality

3. What Cookies We Use

YouTufy currently uses minimal cookies only to remember your preferences, like dismissing the cookie banner. No advertising cookies or trackers are used.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies using your browser settings. You can also clear localStorage from your browser to reset preferences.
